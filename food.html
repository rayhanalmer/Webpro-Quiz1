Bakso Malang

Bakso Malang is a popular Indonesian street food dish originating from the city of Malang in East Java.
It consists of hearty meatball soup served with a flavorful broth, typically made from a mixture of beef and chicken.
The meatballs themselves are usually a combination of ground beef or chicken, tapioca starch, and various seasonings,
resulting in a deliciously tender and bouncy texture.
